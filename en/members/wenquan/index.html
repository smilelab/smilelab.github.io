Quan Wen

School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology, Associate Professor. He holds a bachelor’s degree and a master’s degree from Sichuan University, a doctorate from the University of Texas at Arlington, and a postdoctoral fellow from the Berkeley National Laboratory. Currently serving as a member of many international academic journals, conference committees, conference program committee members, and reviewers. He has published more than 40 papers on SCI/EI retrieval, and he is the first person in charge to host 1 National Natural Science Foundation of China funding project. NSFC research fund for foreign young scholars. The main research interests are target tracking, machine learning, pattern recognition, and medical information processing.
