Zhao Kang

In May 2017, Dr. Kang Zhao graduated from the Department of Computer Science, Southern Illinois University. In July 2017, he joined the University of Electronic Science and Technology. Has published more than 20 papers in top international conferences and journals in the field of artificial intelligence, pattern recognition, information retrieval and data mining, including AAAI, IJCAI, ICDE, CVPR, SIGKDD, ICDM, SDM, CIKM, ACM TIST, ACM TKDD, and Neurocomputing. , Knowledge-Based Systems, etc. He has been invited to serve as a reviewer for top journals in related fields (such as IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, IEEE TNNLS) and as a committee member of meetings (such as AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, ICDM). Welcome to undergraduate and graduate students who want to engage in scientific research and publish papers.
