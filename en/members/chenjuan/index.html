Juan Chen

Juan Chen received a doctoral degree from the University of Bradford in the United Kingdom, a bachelor’s degree from Southwest Normal University, and an associate professor of the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. Published more than 30 periodical and conference papers. The main research projects include: 6 National Natural Science Foundation Projects, 2 basic scientific research business fees for central universities, 4 new experimental construction projects, and 1 EU project. He is a member of the International Journal of Biometrics and Bioinformatics, an international journal editor and a member of the conference proceedings committee of the International Congress on Image and Signal Processing. The main research directions include: machine learning, video tracking, pattern recognition, and biomedical image information.
