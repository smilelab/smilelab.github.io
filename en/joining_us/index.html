UESTC Artificial Intelligence Smart Health Laboratory Recruitment of Full-time Teachers

The artificial intelligence smart health laboratory of the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China is now facing the recruitment of outstanding young scholars at home and abroad for the needs of scientific research. The team’s research focuses on machine learning, data mining technology and its application in the Internet, social network analysis, medical image analysis, security data analysis, and natural language processing.

Recruitment Posts

Associate professor, Lecturer

Concrete Work

We look forward to researchers, who have a strong interest in the direction of the machine learning, social network analysis, knowledge map and big data analysis, to join our team.

Application Requirements

  1. Ph. D in computer, automation, statistics or related fields;
  2. Have machine learning, data mining, statistical learning, optimization, distributed computing, natural language processing, medical image processing, knowledge map and other direction of research experience;
  3. Have scientific research work experience in university, research institutes or Well-known enterprises, have strong research ability, have a major research project working experience is preferred.

Working and living treatment

  1. Provide competitive compensation on the basis of national and school (reference http://www.hr.uestc.edu.cn/pd/index.htm)related standards.
  2. team members will have the opportunity to visit overseas universities to do research or receive training.
  3. Policy and financial support in the Industry-University-Research work.

Application procedures and materials

University of Electronic Science and Technology of China Recruitment schedule

The specific arrangements for the recruitment of full-time teachers in the first half of 2018 are as follows:

1. Post job information：

The Ministry of Human Resources released the recruitment plan for all units in the first half of 2018 (for details, see: University of Electronic Science and Technology, full-time teacher recruitment plan for the first half of 2018)

2. Applicant submits materials (May 28 - June 4)

Please apply to apply for the relevant application materials in accordance with the specific requirements of the notice. Please submit the paper material of the application materials to the office of the College B1-502 before the June 4th (Monday). The e-file will be sent to the e-mail cshr@uestc.edu.cn. Application materials list, see attachment.

3. Applicant review and review of materials (June 4 to June 20)

The first stage: The college reviews the authenticity, accuracy and completeness of the applicant’s basic conditions and performance results to ensure that the relevant supporting materials are available.

The second stage: Organize and develop team (department) appraisal, teaching ability test, professor meeting appraisal, hospital-level professional and technical post appointment team recommendation review and party and government inspection.

4. The college will submit the application materials to the teacher office of the human resources department (June 21)
5. Online publication of applicant’s application materials (three working days) (after June 25th)

If the application materials are falsified, etc., once verified, those who have serious circumstances will be disqualified from applying for entry to our school for the current and future years.

6. Communication evaluation, assessment of school discipline assessment team, and approval of the principal’s office. (after June 25)

UESTC, Artificial Intelligence Smart Health Laboratory

  • Introduction

The main objectives of the research are the basic scientific theories, key technology methods and systems related to big data analysis. At the same time, it promotes the application of big data in medical, mobile, Internet, and education industries. It will focus on research and analysis of big data based on inheriting and tracking big data system architecture and infrastructure (including big data computing architecture, cloud computing technology and platform, data center and green computing), big data acquisition technology and storage technology. Inferential basic science theory, data modeling technology, big data machine learning algorithm, big data reasoning technology, media big data analysis technology, big data distributed computing model and framework, big data visualization technology, and industry-oriented big data analysis and reasoning Technology platform. The research directions mainly include: 1) heterogeneous multi-source big data processing and modeling; 2) big data intelligent computing and analysis technology; 3) big data distributed computing model and system; 4) big data knowledge representation and inference technology.

  • Team leader

Zenglin Xu, Doctors, professors, doctoral tutors, the “Yang Thousand Talents Program” of the Central Organization Department, and the “Thousand Talents Program of Sichuan Province” were selected. Professor Xu Zenglin’s main research interest is machine learning and its application in social network analysis, internet, computational biology, and information security. He has published more than 50 papers in top conferences and publications including NIPS, ICML, IJCAI, AAAI, UAI, CIKM, ICDM, IEEE TPAMI, and IEEE TNN. Google cited more than 1,100 times and published 1 monograph. One of the instructors for the AAAI Best Student Dissertation Nomination Award at the top international conference in the field of intelligence, the best student paper award at the 2016 Asian Machine Learning Conference (ACML) runner-up instructor, obtained by the Asia Pacific Neural Network Association (APNNS) in September 2016 ) The Young Researcher Award. He also gave a teaching report at the International Artificial Intelligence Conference (AAAI) in Toronto in 2012. Professor Zenglin Zeng is a reviewer of JMLR, IEEE TPAMI, and other major journals in the field of machine learning and artificial intelligence; and a fund reviewer of the Hong Kong Education Grants Bureau; served as a member of the program committee of major international conferences in the field of artificial intelligence such as AAAI/IJCAI. ; Served as Chairman of the Organizing Committee for workshops on machine learning and big data research.
